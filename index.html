A bit about this guy...

I started as an Aerospace Engineer.

While still young and full of doubts, I changed my career and became a Graphic Designer. I've followed that path for quite a while now.

I did a lot of school.

I eventually ended up with degrees in Engineering, Computer Science, Technology Management, and half a Master's.

I am big on friends.

Most of my favorite activities include time with my friends. Sharing dinner, movies, drinks or excursions usually involves one or more of our friends.

Life is good that way.

I am a geek.

I try to be the social, friendly, not-offputting kind of geek, but I am unapologetically geek, through and through.
